Pretty washable menstrual pads, hand made with love in the midlands – saving rivers, saving money.
An alternative menstrual product that is hygenic, uncomplicated, safe, eco-friendly and cost effective.
Environmentally friendly menstrual pads made of natural fibres (mostly banana), for women by women.

Will Pink Pads suit you?


  • saves money (at least R50 per month or more) for 3 years
  • need clean water and soap to wash well
  • need outdoor sunlight to dry and sanitise
  • no plastic against your skin causing irritation and sweatiness

    Will My Own Cup suit you?


    • saves money – lasts for 5 years
    • low environmental impact
    • ideal for hiking and camping
    • must be inserted into the vagina
    • clean water needed to wash

    Will Koshighadi pads suit you?


    • same cost as commercial brands
    • looks and feels familiar
    • less impact on the environment (compostable)
